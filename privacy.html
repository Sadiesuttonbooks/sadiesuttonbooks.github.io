Sadie Sutton is the pen name of an independent romance author ("we," "us"). This page explains, in plain language, what we do with your information when you join the reader list.

What we collect

Only your email address, and only when you choose to give it to us by subscribing. We may also see basic, non-identifying analytics (such as whether an email was opened), which our email provider records automatically.

Why we collect it

To send you the free book you requested and occasional news about new releases, sales, and bonus stories. That is all. We do not use your email for anything else.

How it is stored

Your email is stored and managed through our email service provider, MailerLite, which processes it on our behalf under their own privacy and security terms. You can read MailerLite's privacy policy at mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy.

We never sell or share it

We do not sell, rent, or trade your email address to anyone, ever. It is used only to send you our own author emails.

Your choices

Every email we send includes a one-click unsubscribe link. You may leave the list at any time, for any reason, and your address is removed. To have your data deleted, or to ask any question about it, email us at sadiesuttonbooks@outlook.com.

Cookies

Our signup pages may set basic cookies through MailerLite to make the form work and to measure signups. They are minimal and are not used to track you across other websites.

Children

Our books are written for adults, and this list is not directed at anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
